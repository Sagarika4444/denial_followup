Missing/Invalid Authorization

Authorization:

Authorization in medical billing refers to the process of obtaining prior approval from a payer, typically an insurance company, before a healthcare service is provided. This ensures that the service will be covered under the patient's insurance plan. The authorization process involves checking the medical necessity of the service and verifying that the provider is within the insurance network. Obtaining authorization can help prevent denied claims and ensure that healthcare providers receive payment for their services.
